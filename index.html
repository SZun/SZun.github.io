About Me




Hello World! My name is Sam G. Zun and I'd like to welcome you to my personal website! For the past few years I have been self-teaching myself coding through resources such as Codecademy and Free Code Camp, exploring HTML, CSS, Javascript, Java, Ruby and Ruby on Rails. Currently I am a student of the Northwestern University Coding Bootcamp and am thoroughly motivated to begin a career in Full Stack Web Development. After completing this program my goal is to transfer to Northeastern University in Boston, Massachusetts to pursue a bachelor's degree in Computer Science/Economics. Upon completing that bachelor's degree my goal is to attain a masters degree from DePaul University in Software Engineering with a concentration of Software Architecture. Please take a look around my website to get a better understanding of my work or to contact me. And thank you so much for stopping by!
